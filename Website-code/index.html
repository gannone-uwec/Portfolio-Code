ABOUT

CURRICULUM VITAE | PUBLICATIONS | PERSONAL STATEMENT

Eliot N. Gannon is a graduating senior at the University of Wisconsin-Eau Claire with a Bachelor of Arts in Creative Writing and Psychology. He is awaiting decision letters from a list of seventeen Creative Writing MFA programs to which he has already been accepted to Chapman University in CA. He is hoping for an acceptance to one of the fully-funded alternative programs or a generous scholarship or financial aid offer from Chapman. He has a passion for writing stories of literary and genre fiction.
